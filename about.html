About us




The Sandbox Project is an enterprise that primarily works with music production. Music Production involves Playing, Recording and Mastering of Musical instruments as well as vocal music. As a Production Label, We record, as well as make songs. These Songs can be previous Bollywood or Hollywood Song as well as New Compositions Made by the Sandbox team.



Apart from Music Production The Sandbox Project also does Video Shooting and Video Editing. Sandbox is also a platform for breeding artists, performers and creative hearts for developing, publishing and promoting the different talents they possess.


These artists are promoted through Instagram and YouTube as well as through jams and live performances.

In the initialization of The Sandbox Project We're featuring different talents on our social media handles and promoting them, along with arranging jam sessions to discover and promote music and have been successful in engaging properly with the followers.

The Sandbox Project makes its presence felt on YouTube,Fcebook and Instagram currently.



Our Vision

If You've Got Talent, We've Got The Stage

The vision of Sandbox is loud and clear, to promote and develop talents and let the talented ones find their feet on the platform joining them with their audience. Along with others, to make music and musicians, providing a setup for them to play and reach the people.

The sandbox project aims to become a full-fledged music label that produces records create and handles all aspects of music that can be. Apart from providing a platform to budding artist sandbox also used to work with artist that are already rooted within the industry and provide them the best quality of services at the cheapest rates that are possible. In the future the sandbox project aims to become a production house and work with many talents from across the city as well as the country to establish a greater realm of professional as well as passionate music.

We aim to deliver services that are of the best quality and the most creative in a way that is comfortable to both polished artists and upcoming talents as well. Since we were managing the sandbox are also musicians we seem to understand music better and deliver much better content then other production houses available right now.
