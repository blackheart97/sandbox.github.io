What We Do ?

The Sandbox Project is a Multigenred Stage for all kinds of artists skilled in any performing arts

Apart from producing our own industry class music with our very own team of budding artists, we also release music produced by others through our youtube channel and help promote various art forms through our channel as well as other social media platforms.

From recording to playing live we handle all aspects of our music productions ourselves and also mentor and tutor young artists in various aspects.

  • A  network

    We aim to create a strong network of professional as well as semi-professional artists who are willing to collaborate with us as well as work in bigger projects.

  • A  Sanctuary

    In Order to stand alongside any artist we beleive we need to have a sanctuary for protection as well as curation of various art forms that are slowly perishing.

  • Languages No Bar !

    We at the Sandbox Project beleive that there's No Language bar to Arts and support all artists working in different linguistic languages.
